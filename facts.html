Personal Thoughts

  1. Personal Results from the Survey:
    • Ate at the COG through freshman and junior year, totaling 2 years.
    • Ate at the COG usually twice a day, excluding one or two off days. I averaged eating at the COG 10 or more times a week, putting me in the minority.
    • I have no dietary restrictions of note.
    • Like the majority, I lean more towards other on-campus dining options.
    • Again like the majority, I believe that the nutritional value of the food is only sometimes up to standard.
    • In order to save money, I'd prefer getting what I paid for at the COG rather than aiming for healthier alternatives.
    • Albeit, as a major foodie I've still found myself spending a lot outside of campus.
    • Lastly, the COG food does make me fall under the bracket of feeling sometimes good and sometimes bad after a meal.

  2. Experience Working Under Sodexo?:
    • As a caterer I've worked hand-in-hand with the people who work at the COG.
    • I know the facilities they use, and their main kitchen is menacing.
    • The volume of food that gets cooked from the loads of cans, containers, and etc. is manned by a large crew.
    • Luckily, the area is acceptably clean and the cleaning area is widely separated from the cooking area.
    • It does stink in there though.

  3. Final Thoughts?:
    • Because I fully cook for myself now, I prefer foods outside, rather than from inside the COG.
    • The COG food is fine, and does the job but it isn't a top pick.
    • And the food is at least prepped with care for its students.
